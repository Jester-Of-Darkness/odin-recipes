Lasagne Recipe

Credit to Bru-nO the photographer of the image.

This is an easy homemade lasagne that can be prepared ahead of time and saved in the freezer, uncooked, for when you need it. Bake for an extra 45 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 olive oil plus extra for the dish
  • 750g lean beef mince
  • 800g passata or half our basic tomato sauce
  • 200ml hot beef stock
  • 300g fresh lasagne sheets
  • white sauce (find a recipe in the method, or use shop-bought)
  • 125g ball mozzarella torn into thin strips

Method

Step 1

To make the meat sauce, heat 2 tbsp olive oil in a frying pan and cook 750g lean beef mince in two seperate batches for about 10 mins until the mince meat is brown.

Step 2

Pour over 800g passata or half our basic tomato sauce recipe and 200ml hot beef stock. Season to taste.

Step 3

Bring up to the boil, then simmer for 30 mins until the sauce looks rich.

Step 4

Heat the oven to 180C/160C fan/gas 4 and lightly oil an ovenproof dish (about 30 x 20cm).

Step 5

Spoon one third of the meat sauce into the dish, then cover with some fresh lasagne sheets from a 300g pack. Drizzle over roughly 130g ready-made white sauce.

Repeat until you have three layers of pasta. Cover with the remaining 390g white sauce, making sure you can't see any pasta poking through.

Step 6

Put 125g of the torn mozzarella over the top.

Bake for 45 mins until the top is bubbling and golden brown.
